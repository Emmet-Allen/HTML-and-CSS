Mrs.Freda Ocran, RN

Picture of Mrs.Ocran

Mrs. Ocran was loved by all of the nursing staff within Jacobi Hospital NYCHHC. She had the innate ability to make a room spark with joy, along with being able to track down anyone who was missing any of their occupational education; especially PMCS. Mrs. Ocran was the first person many of us within the Behavioral Health Service began their training with, and was a veteran nurse in the field of Psychatric Nursing.
The joy and laughter that she gave to her cohorts in Jacobi will be greatly missed.
